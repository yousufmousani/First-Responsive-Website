SAYLANI INTRODUCTION


Saylani Welfare International Trust has been working since 1999 to improve the conditions of the less privileged, helpless, and handicapped individuals. The organization is working day and night to make life happier, especially for the middle class, lower middle class and even lower class. At time of establishing the organization, the founder of Saylani Welfare International Trust and a few of his associates had made a commitment to serve the distressed people living in Pakistan and abroad in all stages of life. It is a blessing in disguise that today the organization is serving humanity in more than 63 areas of life without any discrimination. The soul, founder and patron of this organization is Hazrat Allama Maulana Muhammad Bashir Farooq. He is also a well-known spiritual person and Islamic scholar in the Islamic world. It is the result of his efforts that today Saylani Welfare International Trust is serving humanity all over the world. The organization started out as a rented house and today has at least 630 branches worldwide. The organization also has more than 630 Dastarkhwan, where thousands of employees are employed. Today, the organization spends more than 7 billion annually on the service and welfare of humanity. Not only this, but more than 63 spheres of life from birth to death (including food, health, education, social welfare, clean water, marriage, mass I.T training, vocational training, assistance to Syrian and Burmese refugees, pilgrim services, school services fees, easy loans, easy employment, hairdressing, medical & diagnostic center, laboratory tests, x-ray, ultrasound, ECG, consultant clinic, mobile dining, mobile clinic, mobile air care clinic & operation theater, and in case of natural disasters aid) our organization is serving humanity. More than 300,000 people are benefited daily by the Saylani. The organization provides vocational training to unskilled people in various fields, renting of poor people houses, school fees and annual expenses of their children, assistance in marriage of their daughters, loan facility for running small scale business, rickshaws and motorbikes for employment, stalls, shop delivery, finger chips machine, sewing machines for women and other items are provided



Saylani Welfare is a prominent non-profit organization that has been making a significant impact on the lives of people in need for several years. Established in 1999 in Karachi, Pakistan, Saylani Welfare's mission is to serve humanity by providing humanitarian aid, education, healthcare, and vocational training to underprivileged individuals and communities. Over the years, the organization has grown exponentially and has expanded its operations to various parts of the country, touching the lives of millions. One of the primary focuses of Saylani Welfare is providing food and meals to those facing hunger and poverty. Through its extensive network of food banks and mobile kitchens, the organization ensures that nutritious food reaches the most vulnerable populations. This includes not only providing meals to the homeless and those in slums but also organizing food distribution drives during emergencies and natural disasters. In addition to its food programs, Saylani Welfare places significant emphasis on education and skill development. The organization runs schools, vocational training centers, and computer literacy programs, empowering the underprivileged with knowledge and skills that can transform their lives. By providing access to quality education and training, Saylani Welfare aims to break the cycle of poverty and create a more sustainable and prosperous future for the beneficiaries. Saylani Welfare also operates several healthcare initiatives, including medical camps, mobile dispensaries, and free medical treatment facilities. These efforts ensure that the impoverished can access basic healthcare services and necessary medical treatment without financial burden. The organization's commitment to healthcare extends beyond physical ailments, as it also provides mental health support and counseling to those in need. Moreover, Saylani Welfare actively engages in humanitarian relief work during disasters, providing immediate aid to affected communities. Whether it's floods, earthquakes, or other calamities, the organization swiftly responds to the crisis, distributing food, clean water, and essential supplies to alleviate suffering and help people rebuild their lives. In conclusion, Saylani Welfare has become a beacon of hope for millions of people in Pakistan, offering a lifeline to those struggling with poverty, hunger, and lack of opportunities. Through its diverse range of initiatives, the organization addresses the fundamental needs of the underprivileged while also working towards long-term solutions. The dedication and tireless efforts of Saylani Welfare and its volunteers have transformed countless lives, making it a vital force for positive change in society.
